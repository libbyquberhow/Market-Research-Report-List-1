<p><p>Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) is essential for providing breathable air in hazardous environments. The market is primarily divided into two types: Open-Circuit SCBA, which expels exhaled air into the atmosphere, making it suitable for short-duration uses like firefighting; and Closed-Circuit SCBA, which recycles exhaled air, allowing for longer usage in confined spaces. Both types are critical for sectors such as firefighting, industrial safety, and military operations, ensuring respiratory protection in various hazardous situations.</p></p>
