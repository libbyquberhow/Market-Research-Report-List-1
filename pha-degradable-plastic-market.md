<p><p>The PHA degradable plastic market includes several types, each defined by their unique properties. PHB (polyhydroxybutyrate) is a natural polymer with excellent biodegradability. PHBV (polyhydroxybutyrate-co-valerate) enhances flexibility and impact resistance. PHBHx (polyhydroxybutyrate-co-hexanoate) exhibits improved thermal properties and elasticity. PHB4B (polyhydroxybutyrate-4-hydroxybutyrate) is known for its high-performance applications. Lastly, "Others" encompasses various blends and copolymers, broadening the market's application in packaging, agriculture, and disposable products, focusing on sustainability and environmental impact reduction.</p></p>
