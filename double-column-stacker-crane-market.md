<p><p>The Double Column Stacker Crane market comprises two primary types: Single Deep Fork and Double Deep Fork. Single Deep Fork stackers utilize a fork that can handle one pallet at a time, ideal for high-density storage while maintaining accessibility. In contrast, Double Deep Fork stackers can accommodate two pallets stacked in-depth, maximizing storage efficiency but requiring more precise maneuvering and space. Both types cater to different warehouse layouts and operational needs, enhancing material handling and storage capabilities.</p></p>
