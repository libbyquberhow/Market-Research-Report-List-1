<p><p>The automatic curved sliding doors market primarily features two types: single sliding doors and bi-parting doors. Single sliding doors consist of one panel that glides open, ideal for spaces with limited width. They provide efficient access while maximizing visual appeal. Bi-parting doors, on the other hand, feature two panels that slide apart from the center, allowing for wider openings and enhanced flow in high-traffic areas. Both types are popular in commercial settings for their ease of use and modern aesthetics.</p></p>
