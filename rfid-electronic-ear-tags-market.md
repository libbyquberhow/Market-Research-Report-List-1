<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li><li>UHF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID electronic ear tags are used for tracking livestock and enhancing farm management. The market is segmented into three main types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF). LF tags operate at 125-134 kHz, offering short-range communication ideal for close-range applications. HF tags, ranging from 13.56 MHz, provide longer read distances and faster data transfer, suitable for diverse uses. UHF tags, operating between 860-960 MHz, enable longer read ranges and support greater data storage, making them ideal for large-scale tracking.</p></p>
