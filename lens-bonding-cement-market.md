<p><ul><li>Epoxy Cement</li><li>Acrylate Cement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lens bonding cement market comprises primarily two types: epoxy cement and acrylate cement. Epoxy cement offers strong adhesion and resistance to environmental factors, making it suitable for precision optics and high-performance applications. In contrast, acrylate cement provides quick curing times and good optical clarity, making it ideal for applications requiring faster processing. Both types cater to various industries, including eyeglasses, cameras, and other optical devices, balancing performance characteristics to meet specific bonding needs in lens manufacturing.</p></p>
