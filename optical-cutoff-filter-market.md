<p><strong>The Optical Cutoff Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shortpass Filter</li><li>Longpass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical cutoff filter market includes shortpass and longpass filters, each serving distinct applications in various industries. Shortpass filters allow wavelengths below a specific cutoff to pass through, effectively blocking higher wavelengths, making them ideal for UV applications and fluorescence microscopy. Conversely, longpass filters permit wavelengths above a certain threshold, blocking shorter wavelengths, suitable for applications like photography and laser systems. Both types enhance imaging quality and precision across fields such as telecommunications, medical devices, and scientific research.</p></p>
