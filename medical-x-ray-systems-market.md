<p><p>The Medical X-ray Systems market can be categorized into several types based on the detector technology used. Amorphous Silicon and Amorphous Selenium (TFT) detectors are widely utilized for their excellent image quality and efficiency; amorphous silicon is common in flat-panel detectors, while amorous selenium is known for its direct conversion of X-rays to electrical signals. CMOS detectors are gaining popularity due to their lower power consumption and compact design. Other technologies in this market include traditional film-based systems and digital imaging solutions.</p></p>
