<p><p>The fiber optical switches market is primarily categorized into single-mode and multimode fiber optical switches. Single-mode fiber optical switches are designed to transmit signals over long distances with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and data center applications. In contrast, multimode fiber optical switches support shorter distances and higher bandwidth, suitable for local area networks and enterprise environments. These switches enhance network performance, providing essential connectivity and flexibility in modern communication systems.</p></p>
