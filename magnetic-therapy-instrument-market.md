<p><ul><li>Magnetostatic Therapy</li><li>Dynamic Magnetic Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic therapy instruments are categorized into two primary types: magnetostatic therapy and dynamic magnetic therapy. Magnetostatic therapy utilizes static magnetic fields to alleviate pain and promote healing by influencing blood flow and cellular functions. In contrast, dynamic magnetic therapy employs varying magnetic fields, creating pulsed or alternating magnetic effects that can enhance tissue oxygenation and stimulate healing processes. Both approaches aim to harness the benefits of magnetism for therapeutic applications, addressing various health conditions and improving overall well-being.</p></p>
